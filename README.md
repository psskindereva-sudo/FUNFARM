# 🐷 Fun Farm 🍎

A small Hay-Day-style farming game you play in your browser.

## ▶️ How to open it (one click)
Double-click **`Fun Farm.app`** — it opens the game in your browser.
(You can also just double-click `index.html`.)

The first time, macOS might ask "are you sure?" — click **Open**.

## How to play
1. 🌱 **Grow crops** — pick a seed, click an empty plot, wait for it to grow, click again to harvest.
2. 🐷 **Feed animals** — buy an animal, click it to feed it your crops.
3. 💰 **Sell animals** — when an animal is fully grown, click it to sell for money.
4. 🥧 **Bake pies** (Level 5) — build the Pie Factory and turn fruit into pies for big money.

## The 5 levels (unlock automatically by what you earn)
| Level | Grow | Feed | Sell | Unlocks at |
|-------|------|------|------|------------|
| 1 | 🍎 Apples | 🐷 Pigs | $70 | start |
| 2 | 🥕 Carrots | 🐄 Cows | $190 | earn **$100** from pigs |
| 3 | 🍓 Strawberries | 🐑 Sheep | $320 | earn **$300** from cows |
| 4 | 🍌 Bananas | 🐵 Monkeys | $450 | earn **$500** from sheep |
| 5 | 🥧 Pie Factory — bake 🍓 & 🍌 pies | — | $55–60 / pie | earn **$800** from monkeys = 🏆 **WIN!** |

After Level 5 you keep farming and baking forever. Progress saves automatically in your browser.

## Want to change the game?
All prices, grow times, and level goals are near the top of the `<script>` in `index.html`, with plain-language comments so you can tweak them.
