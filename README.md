# 🐷 Fun Farm 🍎

A small Hay-Day-style farming game you can play in your browser.

## How to play
Open **`index.html`** (just double-click it) and:

1. 🌱 **Grow crops** — pick a seed, click an empty plot, wait for it to grow, click again to harvest.
2. 🐷 **Feed animals** — buy an animal, click it to feed it your crops.
3. 💰 **Sell animals** — when an animal is fully grown, click it to sell for money.

## Levels (unlock automatically by what you earn)
| Level | Grow | Feed | Sell | Unlocks at |
|-------|------|------|------|------------|
| 1 | 🍎 Apples | 🐷 Pigs | $70 each | start |
| 2 | 🥕 Carrots | 🐄 Cows | $190 each | earn **$100** from pigs |
| 3 | 🍓 Strawberries | 🐑 Sheep | $320 each | earn **$300** from cows |

After Level 3 you can keep farming forever. Your progress saves automatically in your browser.

## Want to change the game?
All the prices, grow times, and goals live near the top of the `<script>` in `index.html` — they have plain-language comments so you can tweak them.
